Check List structure:

The Testing Checklist is a document that describes which features should be tested. Testing Checklists may have different levels of detail.

A Checklist is a catalog of items/tasks that are recorded for tracking. This list could be either ordered in a sequence or could be haphazard.

The Testing Checklist should include:

1. General information about project, author, date, version and etc.
2. a detailed list of tests
3. verification status
4. verification results
